UNIVERSITY STUDENT FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE AND FEE CONCESSION POLICY
---------------------------------------------------------------

1. INTRODUCTION
The University recognizes that financial stress is a significant barrier to academic continuity.
This policy outlines fee concessions, installment options, emergency financial support, and student obligations.

2. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
Fee concessions and installment plans may be considered for:
- Students from low-income households (income certificate required)
- Students belonging to EWS, SC/ST/OBC categories (as per government norms)
- Single-parent households
- Students affected by recent financial shocks such as job loss, medical emergencies, crop failure
- Students recommended by faculty mentors based on psychosocial assessment

3. AVAILABLE SUPPORT MECHANISMS
A. *Fee Installment Plan (FIP)*
   - Allows payment of semester fees in 2–4 installments
   - Late fee penalties may be waived in verified hardship cases

B. *Partial Tuition Fee Waiver*
   - Up to 50% waiver for economically disadvantaged students
   - Requires approval from the Student Welfare Committee (SWC)

C. *Emergency Support Grant*
   - One-time assistance for sudden crises
   - Processed within 5 working days upon verification

D. *Scholarship Counselling*
   - Assistance with applications to NSP, state schemes, NGO scholarships

4. PROCESS FOR APPLICATION
Step 1: Student meets faculty mentor to discuss the situation
Step 2: Student fills “Financial Hardship Request Form”
Step 3: Submit supporting documents (income certificate, medical reports, etc.)
Step 4: Mentor submits recommendation to SWC
Step 5: SWC reviews and communicates decision within 10–15 working days

5. STUDENT RESPONSIBILITIES
- Provide authentic documents
- Apply early, preferably before fee deadlines
- Maintain satisfactory academic engagement unless exempted

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
All financial information is kept confidential and shared only with authorized staff.

7. APPEAL PROCESS
Students may appeal rejected applications by submitting additional documentation.
